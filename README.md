# 📊 Subscription Upgrades & Cross-Selling Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer subscription behavior to identify opportunities for upgrades and cross-selling. The goal is to help businesses increase revenue by understanding customer patterns and recommending targeted strategies.

## 🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer subscription patterns
Identify upgrade opportunities
Detect cross-selling potential
Provide actionable business recommendations

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas, NumPy
Matplotlib, Seaborn

## 📈 Project Workflow
Data Collection & Understanding
Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Customer Behavior Analysis
Feature Engineering
Identification of Upgrade & Cross-Selling Opportunities
Business Insights & Strategy Recommendations

## 📊 Key Insights
Identified high-value customers likely to upgrade
Discovered patterns for successful cross-selling
Segmented customers based on subscription behavior

## 🚀 Results
Generated data-driven strategies to increase revenue
Highlighted key customer segments for targeted marketing
Improved understanding of customer lifecycle

## 💡 Business Impact
This project helps businesses:
Increase subscription revenue
Improve customer lifetime value (CLV)
Enhance cross-selling effectiveness
Optimize marketing campaigns
